Cloud Services

The term "cloud computing" is everywhere. In the simplest terms, cloud computing means storing and accessing data and programs over the Internet instead of your computer's hard drive. The cloud is just a metaphor for the Internet.

Usage

Using the cloud as a form of storage is the future. Industry leaders such as Dropbox, iCloud and Google Drive are changing the world of storage through cloud technology. Cloud can offer you the possibility of storing your files and accessing, storing and retrieving them from any web-enabled interface. The web services interfaces are usually simple. At any time and place you have high availability, speed, scalability and security for your environment.

Growth

The cloud offers a flexible way for businesses to accommodate both unpredictable demand and planned growth more cost effectively. It can also be used as a platform for delivering new services and accelerating your time-to-market. One of the aspects offered by leveraging cloud computing is the ability to tap into vast quantities of both structured and unstructured data to harness the benefit of extracting business value.
